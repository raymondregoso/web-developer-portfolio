E-COMMERCE

An E-Commerce backend refers to the server-side or behind-the-scenes components of an electronic commerce (E-Commerce) website or application. It encompasses the software, databases, and infrastructure that power the online store and enable it to function. The E-Commerce backend is responsible for managing various aspects of an online store, including product inventory, user accounts, payment processing, order management, and more.
